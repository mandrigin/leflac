# LE FLAC 1.2.0 — Up Next priority bus

LF-1 now has an explicit scheduled future ahead of both ORDER and RNG. It is a
real Media3 timeline segment, not a UI-only shadow queue, so the phone,
notification controls, and projected Android Auto all operate on one order.

## Highlights

- Hold a track to enter UP NEXT selection, tap more tracks in FIFO order, then
  press `[QUEUE]`. Normal taps remain play-now.
- New `NEXT nn` ledger tab with queued-row ordinals, per-item removal,
  clear-all, an empty-state guide, haptics, and accessibility labels.
- Scheduled items take priority over ORDER/RNG and survive rail changes,
  Activity recreation, service resumption, and car reconnects.
- Remove/clear leave the underlying rail intact; once a scheduled item is
  consumed, its first later natural repeat is removed. Repeated scheduling is
  idempotent.
- Song and mix pools remain separate. While a long mix is active, phone CUE+
  remains an intra-mix cue control and scheduled mixes apply at the next file
  transition.
- Play-now selections intentionally start a new context and clear the explicit
  schedule.

Android Auto needs no extra browse shelf: hosts receive UP NEXT through the
standard shared playback timeline and may expose it on their normal queue page.
Physical Honda e queue-page and steering-wheel acceptance remain live checks in
the [operator guide](https://github.com/mandrigin/leflac/blob/v1.2.0/docs/ANDROID_AUTO.md).
